Rescue Me Redesign

Rescume.org is a non-profit website built to help people find animals in local shelters to adopt. Client needed a website redesign that would increase traffic and usage of their site and increase donations made to the non-profit organziation.

Research
User Research

We spoke with the target audience to see their preferences when adopting an animals. This helped us create our user persona.

Heuristic Evaluation

As a team we went through the existing website, highlighting their flaws and areas for improvement

User Flows and Story Boarding

After the previous two parts, we went through and created a user flow, user journey, and a story board to see how we wanted the flow of the website to go.

Brainstorming

The team came up with multiple fuctions and ideas but we had to narrow them down to the most important. We chose a search fucntion, donations , and sucess stories.

Problem Statement

Potential pet adopters need an online resource that helps them navigate to the best match for their lifestyle and personality. RescueMe.Com was re-designed to direct users to a better search process. We have observed that potential adopters are unaware of online resources that can guide them to their desired pet. These adopters will be successful in finding a match by using selected filters to specify their needs during the search process.

Final Redesign
